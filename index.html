About the event?

International Conference on Engineering , Technology and Management for Sustainable Development(ICETMSD-2018) will be held at DBGI, Dehradun on October 26-27, 2018.

Call for Paper

The conference will cover a wide range of issues related to the Engineering, Technology and Management Problems. It will conduct for research for working in universities, colleges and industry who are interested in enhancing skills.

Registration

In addition, our list of key note and invited speakers includes funding agency sponsors, key industry players, and top scientists from the international community.
